Rambunctious Afghan Hound — SniffNotes

SniffNotes is a tiny, playful shop for scratch-and-sniff postcards and stickers inspired by our Three Rambunctious Afghan Hounds. Wait until you see how accurate these scratches take you.

We keep the catalog small so theres no bs. Simply scratch, sniff, and wag your tail.

How it works

  • Fetch a card or sticker that matches your houndy spirit.
  • Scratch gently to release the scent notes.
  • Share it, stick it, but most importantly, whiff it.

What we make

  • Wind-Sprint Sticker: mint · citrus · clean musk
  • Velvet Coat Postcard: iris · vanilla · suede
  • Regal Prowl Postcard: incense · cedar · oud

Small batches, refreshed whenever the Afghan hound feels like it. No accounts. No upsells. Just whiffs.
