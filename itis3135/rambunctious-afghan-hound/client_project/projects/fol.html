Fruits of Labor — June 18th, 2025

Sequence

Concept & Direction

A visual meditation on the quiet transactions we make to keep moving. These portraits depict the dualities of pleasure and burden, exploring how vice—both subtle and overt—becomes a crutch, a ritual, a moment of stillness between cycles of pressure. In a world demanding constant productivity, even our indulgences begin to feel earned, rationed, or haunted. This series contemplates the emotional residue of effort: how we seek balance, how we carry burnout with grace, and how the things that sustain us can also rot us from within. The images do not condemn or celebrate—but observe. The “fruits” of our labor are often not rewards, but coping mechanisms dressed in softness, smoke, and silence.
